    Introducing the POIs

    Hang Seng Management College is a university-level institution in Hong Kong that offers undergraduate programs and postgraduate degree programmes. Originally the Hang Seng School of Commerce, the institution reformed into the current HSMC in 2010, and is now applying to become a private university in Hong Kong. The HSMC Residential Colleges, constructed in 2015, are part of the HSMC campus and are about 5 minutes away from the main campus. The residential college system of HSMC aims for students to develop self-improvement thinking, inter-personal relationships and also self-discipline.


    恆生管理學院學生宿舍（沙田）

    恆生管理學院是香港一所具大學水平的教育機構，提供多元化的學士及碩士學位課程。恒管於2010年由恒生商學書院改組成立，現正申請成為私立大學。恆生管理學院學生宿舍建於2015年，是恒管園區的一部分，距離主園區約五分鐘路程。恆生管理學院的宿舍系統以發展學生的自我提升思想，人際關係與自律。

    Ever since its launch back in 2005, the fantasy, fairy tale-like Hong Kong Disneyland has been well received by locals and tourists all around the world. There are seven main theme lands in the park, which are Main Street, U.S.A., Adventureland, Fantasyland, Tomorrowland, Toy Story Land, Grizzly Gulch, and Mystic Point, each with its own charm. Visitors can also meet their favourite Disney friends. Other than exciting amusement rides, the park also has live-action shows, parades and firework shows to provide a fun and unforgettable experience for visitors.


    迪士尼樂園（香港））

    香港迪士尼樂園於2005年開幕，被譽為童話故事般的夢幻國度，備受本地及外國遊客歡迎的旅遊必到之處。園內有7大主題園區（美國小鎮大街、探險世界、幻想世界、明日世界、反斗奇兵大本營、灰熊山谷及迷離莊園），各有特色。除了刺激的機動遊戲，樂園更以真人表演的娛樂節目、花車巡遊和煙花匯演著稱。有別於在其他城市的主題樂園，香港迪士尼樂園洋溢著濃厚的美國的氣息之餘也不乏本地特色，如夢想花園。在夢想花園中，遊客可以在幻想世界的奇妙花園中與你最喜愛的迪士尼朋友見面，共度奇妙時刻。

    As an important landmark of Hong Kong, the Tsing Ma Bridge was opened to traffic in 1997. Spanning an incredible 2.16 km, it is the world’s longest suspension bridge with two decks for road and rail traffic, and the world’s eleventh longest suspension bridge. After mounting a spiralling road to a cylindrical observatory, visitors can gain a close-up view of the Tsing Ma Bridge, Kap Shui Mun Bridge and TIng Kau Bridge from a high vantage point.


    青馬大橋（葵青）

    香港的一個重要地標—青馬大橋於1997年開幕，長約2.16公里，是全球最長的行車鐵路雙用懸索吊橋，以及全球第11長的懸索吊橋。從螺旋式的小路繞着圓柱形的觀景台而上，遊人可以從近距離或高角度，俯瞰青馬大橋、汲水門大橋及汀九橋。

    Situated in Northeast New Territories, Lai Chi Wo was one of its wealthiest villages. Built beside the sea next to Pan Pui Teng, Lai Chi Wo was named after its abundant growth/harvesting/production of the Lychee fruit. On a clearing in front of the village, two cannons are placed facing the sea. According to urban legends, the cannons were used to defend and warn against pirate attacks. Besides, Lai Chi Wo is also famous for its rich biodiversity, and is a renowned spot for geological and ecological tours. Red sedimentary rocks, lush mangrove forests Dwarf, Hong Kong’s largest patch of Dwarf eelgrass Zostera japonica seaweed-bed, lively mudskippers and fiddler crabs on swamps are among Lai Chi Wo’s ecology, attesting its high educational value.


    荔枝窩（北區）

    荔枝窩位於新界東北部，是昔日新界東北群村最富庶的鄉村之一。村落臨海而建，背靠攀背頂，因盛產荔枝而得名。村前對開空地放置了兩門炮口面海的鐵炮，據說是因為要防止海盜來襲，以示警告和防禦。除此之外，荔枝窩亦以生物之多樣性著稱，是地質生態遊的最佳地點之一。紅色的沉積岩，泥灘上長滿茂密的紅樹林，香港東北面水域的最大片矮大葉藻海草床，以及活躍在泥灘的招潮蟹和彈塗魚等等都只是當地生態中的寥寥數種，可見此地具有很高的教育價值。

    Inaugurated in 1979, the Clear Water Bay Country Park in Southeast New Territories is famed for its green hills and clear waters, along with its peculiar rock structures. For instance, Tai Leng Tung surrounds the cape of east Clear Water Bay, allowing tourists to contemplate the entire island. Striding up along Lung Ha Wan, one can observe weathered rocks on elevated grounds, and Igneous rocks at lower altitudes, bearing witness to its grandeur. Subsequently, there are many challenging trails for eager hikers. Moreover, Clear Water Bay is home to a wide array of botanical and avian species, such as the Ficus hispida, Liquidammintraveltime formosana Hance, Garcinia oblongifolia and black-eared kite.


    清水灣郊野公園（西貢）

    成立於1979年的清水灣郊野公園位於新界東南部，以青山綠水、奇石嶙峋見稱。例如，大嶺峒包圍清水灣東面海角，能俯瞰整個海岸。沿龍蝦灣小徑往上走，高處是因風化侵蝕而露出的岩石，低處則是山岳下的火成岩，氣勢磅礡。不僅如此，那裡還有很多郊遊徑供行山愛好者逐一挑戰。此外，當地的植物及鳥類品種繁多，如對葉榕、球楓、黃牙果、黑耳鳶（麻鷹)。 。

    Man Ho Temple is located in the busy district of Sheung Wan, and for its long history, it is now a declared monument. The temple worships Man Tai, the civil and literature god; Mo Tai, the martial god, Pao Kung, the god of justice, and also Chenghuangshen. Inside the temple are multiple artefacts, including a bronze bell from the Wing dynasty, and a cart for officials made in 1862. Every autumn there is an autumn festival held in the temple, where prayers are made for the citizens of Hong Kong.


    文武廟（大埔）

    文武廟位處上環的鬧市中，因其歷史之悠久，被鑑定為法定古蹟。廟宇香火鼎盛，是虔誠的信徒祈求祝願的地方。廟裏供奉著「文帝」、「武帝」、包公，以及城隍，存放著兩件逾百年的歷史文物：清朝道光年的銅鐘，以及一台1862年造成的官轎。每逢秋天時節，文武廟都會舉行盛大的秋季典禮，為香港的普羅大眾祈福。

    Opened in 2009, Noah’s Ark is a theme park which is a full-size replica of the real Noah’s Ark in the Bible. Popular with both international and local visitors, the park has theme areas in within, and through using animal fables, educates visitors on positive values. A 180-degree cinema introduces the biblical story of Noah’s Ark, and with a series of interactive games on the topic of ‘life’, Noah’s Ark allows visitors to review on their thoughts on life from multiple perspectives.


    挪亞方舟（馬灣）

    挪亞方舟是於2009年開幕的主題公園。方舟博覽館內設有主題性展覽，透過動物寓言故事給遊客帶來正面的啟發。博覽館內也設有180度環迴影院，介紹聖經中挪亞方舟的故事。方舟生命教育館則透過一系列以生命教育為主題的體驗性遊戲，讓遊客從多方面思考生命的價值。方舟花園展出67對栩栩如生的動物雕塑。

    Said to be the backyard of the New Territories, Lung Kwu Tan is the furthest point on Hong Kong’s western coastline. With a beach like a crescent moon, it is a great vantage point for the viewing of sunsets. The nearby Urmston Road is also a location where Chinese White Dolphins often appear, and is a must-go location for photographers.     龍鼓灘（屯門）

    被譽為新界的後花園的龍鼓灘是香港西面海岸線最邊的地方。海灘猶如半月，是欣賞落霞的好地方。附近的龍鼓水道更是中華白海豚經常出沒的地方，是攝影發燒友的必去之地。此外，那裡娛樂設施完善，除了燒烤場、小型活動賽車場，遊客還可以到海上玩花式滑水，是週末的好去處。

    Lau Fau Shan is a popular site for tourists, famous for its fresh sealistofdestinationsd and beautiful scenery.     流浮山（元朗）

    流浮山以其出產的海鮮和觀賞夕陽晚景聞名。由於該處遠離市區，鄰近海邊，故能清晰觀賞日落，吸引眾多的攝影愛好者前往。與此同時，其美味新鮮的海鮮也吸引了眾多食客前往品嚐。

    The Kowloon Walled City Park, as of its coastal location, was a military stronghold from the fifteenth to twentieth century, and was a place full of crime and vice. The Walled City was demolished in the 1990s under agreement of the government, but its buildings and features have been preserved since then as a park for nearby residents. Free guided tours have been arranged to provide detailed information and explanations of the park's history and features, and also includes walk throughs of the Exhibition Rooms and Outdoor Display Area.


    九龍城寨公園（九龍城）

    因其優越的沿海地理位置，九龍寨城公園的前身成為了十五至二十世紀重要的軍事據點，卻同時是罪惡的溫床。在九十年代，它在政府的同意下被迫拆卸，但其建築風格和特色都融入了新建的公園以作紀念。該處提供免費而詳盡的導遊團服務，當中包括遊覽室外展廳和展覽室的行程。

    The Wilson Trail has been a favourite among hikers in Hong Kong for its spectacular scenery and landscapes of Victoria Harbour and Tseung Kwan O. It extends from Yau Tong to Tseng Lan Shue, passing through Devil’s Peak and Ma Yau Tong. The entire Wilson trail takes around four hours to complete; however, Stage 3 of the trail only covers Chiu Keng Wan Shan, Ng Kwai Shan and Pau Toi Shan in Kowloon East. Another name of this mountain range is ‘Little Pat Sin’, as the rises and falls resembles that of Pat Sin Leng in Tai Po.’


    衛奕信徑第三段（觀塘）

    衛奕信徑第三段以優美怡人的維港和將軍澳景色著稱，亦是香港的遠足勝地。它橫跨油塘至井欄樹村，穿過魔鬼山和馬游塘。整段行山徑需要大約四小時完成，第三段行山徑的範圍覆蓋因山的高低起伏貌似大埔八仙嶺而被譽為「小八仙」的山脈—照鏡環山、五桂山、九龍東的炮台山。

    Before reclamation development, Sham Shui Po was formerly by the sea where a lot of fishermen resided. They worshipped Tin Hau, the goddess of the sea, and believed in her supernatural power to keep them safe at sea. Tin Hau Temple was built facing the seashore for good Feng Shui, meaning good luck. However, now it is buried in the middle of skyscrapers and high-rise buildings. The Tin Hau Temple houses not only Tin Hau, but also Tai Sui, the sixty gods of time, Kwun Yum, the goddess of mercy, and Pao Kung, the god of justice.


    天后廟（深水埗）

    在填海項目開始前，深水埗鄰近海邊，故有不少漁民定居於此。他們供奉天后，又名海神娘娘，並相信她能保佑漁民平安出海歸來。天后廟因風水的關係面朝大海，寓意好運。如今，天后廟已悄然藏身於高樓大廈中，沒有了這重意義。除了供奉天后娘娘，天后廟也供奉太歲、觀音和包公。

    One of the most famous shrines in Hong Kong, Wong Tai Sin Temple is a Taoist Temple situated on the southern side of Lion Rock in the north of Kowloon. One of the reasons for its fame is the practice of Kau Cim, which is a form of prayer which supports the belief of ‘what you request is what you get’. The Temple is graded as a Grade I historic building. At the beginning of each new year, on Wong Tai Sin’s birthday on the twenty third of the eighth lunar month, and also during the Chinese New Year holidays, the temple is visited by many for praying to the gods.


    黃大仙祠（黃大仙）

    香港最著名的寺廟之一暨一級歷史建築—黃大仙祠是一座座落於九龍東獅子岩南面的道家寺廟。寺廟的獨特之處是有求籤這一項習俗。求籤是一種類似祈禱，建基於心想事成的基礎的形式。每逢農曆八月廿三（黃大仙的生辰）或農曆新年假期，眾多虔誠的信徒便會前往黃大仙祠供奉神明。

    Kowloon Park was once an army fortress, the site of the Whitfield mintraveltimeracks of the British army, but was handed over from the military in 1970 to be redeveloped and opened to the public. It now is one of the largest public parks in Hong Kong, and a common place of recreation for nearby residents. There was also a former battery in the northwestern part of the park. Gardens, museums, lakes, an aviary, sporting facilities, and also the Avenue of Stars are located in the park. The Hong Kong Heritage Discovery Centre and Health Education Exhibition and Resource Centre are also within the park’s premises.


    九龍公園（尖沙咀）

    九龍公園前身為英國軍隊的軍事堡壘，於且1970重建並對外開放。如今，它是香港最大的公共公園之一，亦是附近居民的康樂場所。園內有花園、博物館、湖泊、鳥舍、體育設施、炮台，以及馳名遠方的星光大道。衛生教育展覽及資料中心也位於園內。

    Hong Kong City Hall, located at Edinburgh Place, is a place that provides municipal services to the public, including halls for performing and libraries.     香港大會堂（中環）

    鄰近愛丁堡廣場的香港大會堂是一個提供多方面的市政服務，如圖書館或供表演用的展覽廳。昔日的大會堂多為官員宣誓就職的會場，而現在則多用於為劇場或樂團提供表演場地，或舉辦文藝活動，如香港藝術節或香港國際電影節。

    The Noonday Gun is mounted near the Causeway Bay Typhoon Shelter on Hong Kong Island, and used to serve as a formal naval artillery piece. The gun is fired everyday at noon, hence the name ‘Noonday Gun. During the Japanese occupation of Hong Kong, the Japanese Imperial Army dismantled the gun and it was lost, however after Hong Kong was regained by Britain later in 1945, a new gun was built to continue the tradition.


    午炮（銅鑼灣）

    怡和午炮位於香港灣仔區銅鑼灣避風塘的岸邊，前身為海軍火砲，並於每天香港時間中午12時鳴放禮炮，故名為怡和午炮。1941年，日軍佔領香港，將大炮奪去，至今亦下落不明，1945年英國海軍向怡和洋行送贈一門大炮，使傳統能夠得以延續。

    Ocean Park is a marine themed animal theme park cum amusement park, located in Wong Chuk Hang and Nam Long Shan on Hong Kong Island.     海洋公園（南區）
